Our Program

We Offer An Exclusive Program For Kids

EARLY BUDS

The Early Buds Program nurtures young children through play-based learning and interactive activities. We focus on language, cognitive growth, social skills, and motor development. Our approach encourages creativity, curiosity, and confidence. By building essential skills, we lay a strong foundation for lifelong learning. Every child blossoms in a supportive and engaging environment.

Age: 6 to 12 Months

TINY TODDLERS

The Tiny Toddlers Program is a fun and engaging preschool experience designed for young children to explore the world around them. Through play, songs, and interactive activities, we help toddlers develop foundational skills in language, motor coordination, and socialization. Our nurturing environment encourages curiosity and creativity, laying the groundwork for a love of learning.

Age: 1 to 3 Years

KINDER GARDEN

Our Kinder Garden Program provides a safe and vibrant learning environment for young children. We focus on early literacy, math, and social skills to ease the transition to primary school. Hands-on activities and creative play nurture curiosity, confidence, and independence. Interactive learning fosters a love for discovery and growth. We build a strong foundation for lifelong academic success.

Age: 3+ Years

SENIOR KG

Our Senior KG(Kinder Garden) Program provides a dynamic learning environment for young children. We focus on early literacy, math, and social skills to prepare them for primary school. Hands-on activities and creative play encourage curiosity and independence. Interactive learning fosters confidence and a love for discovery. We build a strong foundation for future academic success.

Age: 4+ Years

DAY CARE

Our Day Care Program provides a safe, caring, and engaging environment for children while parents are at work. With a focus on both learning and fun, we offer a mix of structured activities and free play to support children's social, emotional, and cognitive development. Our dedicated staff ensures each child receives the attention and care they need, making it a home-away-from-home experience.

Age: 6 Months to 10 Years

FUN ARENA

Fun Arena is a dynamic play and learning space designed for children to explore, create, and have fun. Packed with interactive games, creative activities, and exciting adventures, it promotes physical, cognitive, and social development in a safe and joyful environment. Perfect for sparking curiosity and building new friendships, Fun Arena is where kids have a blast while learning and growing!

Age: Upto 10 Years
